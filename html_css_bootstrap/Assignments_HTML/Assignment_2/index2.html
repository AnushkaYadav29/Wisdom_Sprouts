My hobby is crafting macrame. Macrame is an art of creating decorative items using knotting techniques. I enjoy making wall hangings, keychains, and plant holders.

Crafting macrame helps me relax and improve my creativity. It allows me to focus and express my ideas through handmade designs. I feel happy when I complete a new piece.


This hobby also helps me learn patience and attention to detail. I like experimenting with new patterns and designs in macrame.
