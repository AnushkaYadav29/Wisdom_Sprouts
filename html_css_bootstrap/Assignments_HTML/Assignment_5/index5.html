My Favorite Hobby

Why I Like It

My favorite hobby is crafting macrame. I like macrame because it allows me to create beautiful handmade items using simple knots. It helps me relax, improves my creativity, and makes me feel happy when I complete a design.

How I Spend Time Doing It

I usually spend my free time doing macrame crafting in the evening or on weekends. I practice different knotting techniques and make items like wall hangings, keychains, and decorative pieces.

Daily Routine

My daily routine starts early in the morning when I wake up and get ready for the day. I complete my basic activities and have breakfast before starting my studies. Throughout the day, I focus on my work and manage my time properly. In the evening, I relax and spend time doing macrame crafting, and listening to music. At night, I have dinner with my family and prepare for the next day before going to bed. This routine helps me stay organized, disciplined, and creative every day.
