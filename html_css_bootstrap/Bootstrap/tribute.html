Savitribai Phule

January 3, 1831 – March 10, 1897

About Savitribai Phule

Savitribai Phule was India’s first female teacher and a pioneer of women's education. She fought against caste discrimination and worked for social equality.

Early Life

Savitribai was born on January 3, 1831 in Maharashtra. She was married at a young age to Jyotirao Phule who later educated her. She became India's first female teacher in 1848.
